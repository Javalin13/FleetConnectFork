GDPR Compliance & Your rights

Because Ryz3n does not collect personal data through this website (no forms, no tracking, no user accounts), the processing falls under the GDPR exceptions for purely incidental or non‑automated processing. Nevertheless, we value transparency and inform you of your rights under the GDPR:

What happens to emails?

When you send an email to info@ryz3n.com or privacy@ryz3n.com, it is answered manually. Email addresses are not automatically stored in databases or CRM systems. Correspondence is kept only as long as necessary to handle your request, unless a legal retention period applies (e.g., 7 years for accounting purposes). Your email is never sold or shared with third parties.

Your rights under the GDPR:

  • Right of access: You may request at any time what data we process about you. Since we do not collect data, our answer will be that there is no personal data, except for any email correspondence.
  • Right to rectification: If any information does come to us (e.g., via email), you may request correction.
  • Right to erasure (right to be forgotten): You may request deletion of email correspondence, provided no legal retention obligation applies (e.g., statutory 7‑year period for accounting records).
  • Right to restriction of processing: You may ask us to temporarily stop processing your data.
  • Right to data portability: Where applicable, you may receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to object: You may object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to lodge a complaint: You may file a complaint with the Belgian Data Protection Authority (GBA).

For all privacy questions or access/erasure requests: privacy@ryz3n.com — this email address is handled by Ryz3n's Data Protection Officer. Response within 15 working days.

Cookie Policy · No banner needed

Ryz3n places no tracking cookies, no marketing cookies, no analytical cookies. Exception: strictly functional session cookies (only for technical reasons, such as remembering language preference or navigation status). These cookies contain no personal data and expire automatically when you close your browser.

Because no non‑functional cookies are used, there is no cookie banner on the site. You are not bothered with consent windows, in line with our ‘no‑nonsense’ B2B philosophy. You can always adjust your browser settings to clear all cookies.

📌 Based on the ePrivacy Directive & GDPR, no consent is required for functional cookies. Our cookie policy is fully compliant.
