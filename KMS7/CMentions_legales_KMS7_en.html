Any booking is made via the client portal, by phone or by email. A free quote is sent within 10 minutes. The booking is confirmed after validation of the quote and acceptance of these T&C. Article 3 – Rates & payment

Our prices are calculated on a per‑kilometre basis (€1.50/km) possibly increased by a supplement according to the chosen vehicle (Break +€5, Exclusive +€15, MiniVan +€10).
