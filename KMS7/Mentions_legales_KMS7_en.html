General terms and conditions of sale – Professional clients (B2B)

Article 1 – Scope
These terms apply to all passenger transport services provided by KMS7 for companies, associations and self-employed professionals.

Article 2 – Booking and quote
Any booking is confirmed in writing (email, platform) after acceptance of the quote. A unique booking number is assigned. For regular accounts, a framework agreement may be established.

Article 3 – Rates and invoicing
Prices are calculated according to distance, duration and selected options (vehicle category, overtime, waiting time). Belgian VAT applies. Monthly invoice for professional accounts with net payment term of 30 days. Late penalties: 10% of the amount.

Article 4 – Cancellation and modification
Free cancellation up to 4 hours before the pick‑up time. Beyond that, 50% of the amount is charged. In the event of a no‑show, the entire ride is due.

Article 5 – Liability and insurance
KMS7 has professional liability insurance covering material and bodily damage in accordance with Belgian law. In the event of a delay beyond our control (force majeure, strike, exceptional traffic jam), our liability shall not exceed the refund of the service.

Article 6 – Customer obligations
The customer agrees to provide accurate information (address, time) and to be ready at the agreed time. Any excess luggage must be reported at the time of booking.

Article 7 – Disputes and applicable law
These terms are governed by Belgian law. In the event of a dispute, an amicable solution will be sought before any action before the courts of Brussels.

European regulatory framework applicable to the taxi, VTC and limousine sector

KMS7 carries out its passenger transport activities with a driver in accordance with the following European principles and texts:

1. Free movement of services (Directive 2006/123/EC)

This directive guarantees the freedom of establishment and the free movement of services within the EU. KMS7 respects the conditions of access to the Belgian market and may carry out occasional services for clients residing in other Member States.

2. Regulations (EC) No 1071/2009 and 1073/2009

Although mainly applicable to coach carriers, the principles of good repute, financial capacity and professional competence are transposed by Belgium for VTC/taxis. KMS7 guarantees that its drivers have the required certificates (training, criminal record extract).

3. Rights of passengers with reduced mobility (Regulation (EU) No 1107/2006)

KMS7 provides an accessible service on request: adapted vehicles, assistance for persons with reduced mobility, discriminatory refusal of transport prohibited.

4. GDPR (Regulation (EU) 2016/679)

Customers' personal data (professional and private) is processed in accordance with European requirements: consent, defined purpose, right to be forgotten, data security.

5. Regulation (EC) No 561/2006 (driving time)

Although not compulsory for vehicles with fewer than 10 seats, KMS7 applies internal rest and safety rules that exceed the legal minima to ensure optimal safety.

6. Directive 2009/103/EC (motor vehicle liability insurance)

KMS7 takes out insurance that complies with European minimum amounts (cover for material and bodily injury) and provides a certificate during any inspection.

7. Regulation (EU) 2015/758 (eCall)

All recent vehicles in the fleet are equipped with the in‑vehicle emergency call system, improving passive safety.

8. Regulation (EU) 2024/… (Mobility Package – VTC adaptations)

The new directives on digital platforms and the posting of drivers are being closely monitored by KMS7. We are committed to complying with legislative developments to maintain a high‑quality, fully legal service.

Conclusion: KMS7 operates in strict compliance with Belgian and European law, to guarantee its professional and private clients a quality, transparent and secure service.
